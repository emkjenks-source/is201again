My Thoughts

Ballet was not just a hobby for me. It was a passion that shaped my life. Every show I did fueled my love for the sport. It is elegant, strong, and mesmerizing. Ballet is my life and I will forever be grateful for the lessons that I have learned from it. My favorite part of ballet was being on stage performing. When I embrace the character I am playing, it is like every stress in my falls away and I can just play this elegant part. Ballet saved my mental health in high school and allowed me to forget about my anxiety about school and life.


The video below shows my favorite Prima Ballerina, Marianela Nunez, performing one of my favorite scences in the ballet 'Sleeping Beauty'. This is notoriously one of the hardest pieces to perform in all of Ballet because of its intense balances and the endurance that it takes. She is a dancer with the Royal Opera in England, one of the most famous Theatres in the world.
